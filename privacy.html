Privacy Policy

Effective date: 09/07/2026 Company: Aquesa Solutions Private Limited

This Privacy Policy explains how Aquesa Solutions Private Limited ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, stores, and protects personal data when you visit our website, join our waitlist, or communicate with us about our planned product. Our product is currently in a pre-launch stage. This Privacy Policy applies to our website, landing pages, forms, waitlist, and pre-launch communications.

01

Current Stage: Waitlist Only

At this stage, we are collecting information only for pre-signup, waitlist, research, product validation, and launch communication purposes.

Our waitlist form is hosted through Google Forms. When you click the waitlist button on our website, you may be redirected to a Google Forms page where you can submit your waitlist information.

We are not currently providing the full product to waitlist users. We are not asking you to connect your AI accounts, upload AI histories, submit private prompts, store operational memory, connect third-party tools, or provide API keys through the waitlist form.

Please do not submit sensitive personal data, confidential business data, passwords, API keys, private AI prompts, proprietary documents, or third-party personal data through the waitlist form.

02

Personal Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal data when you voluntarily provide it:

  • name;
  • email address;
  • Discord ID;
  • source of information about our product.

If you submit the waitlist form through Google Forms, the information you enter in the form may be collected, stored, and managed through Google Forms, Google Sheets, Google Drive, and related Google services used by us for waitlist operations.

We may also automatically collect limited technical information (directly/indirectly) when you visit our website, such as:

  • IP address;
  • browser type;
  • device type;
  • pages visited;
  • approximate location derived from IP address;
  • referral source;
  • date and time of visit;
  • cookie or analytics identifiers, if enabled.
03

Data We Do Not Collect at Waitlist Stage

At the waitlist stage, we do not intentionally collect:

  • AI chat histories;
  • AI prompts or outputs;
  • files or documents for AI memory;
  • API keys;
  • passwords;
  • third-party AI account credentials;
  • production workspace data;
  • sensitive personal data unless you voluntarily include it in a message to us.

If you submit such data accidentally, you may contact us and request deletion.

04

How We Use Personal Data

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • to manage the waitlist;
  • to contact you about product updates, early access, beta access, launch announcements, or related information;
  • to understand demand, user segments, and product use cases;
  • to improve our website, positioning, and product roadmap;
  • to respond to your messages or requests;
  • to maintain security and prevent misuse;
  • to comply with legal obligations.

We do not use waitlist data to make automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects about you.

05

Consent and Lawful Processing

When you submit the waitlist form, you consent to our processing of your personal data for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

You may withdraw your consent or unsubscribe from communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link in our emails, if available, or by contacting us at info@aquesa-solutions.com.

Withdrawal of consent will not affect processing already completed before withdrawal.

06

AI Training and Model Improvement

At the waitlist stage, we do not use your waitlist personal data, messages, or feedback to train public AI models.

If we later offer a product that processes AI memory, prompts, histories, files, branches, embeddings, or connected workspace data, we will provide updated terms, a product-specific privacy policy or notice, and relevant user controls before collecting or processing such data.

Unless we clearly ask for separate permission, we will not use your private product data to train our own or third-party AI models.

07

Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to operate the website, understand traffic, measure campaign performance, prevent abuse, and improve user experience.

If we use analytics, advertising pixels, or similar tracking tools, we will disclose them in this Privacy Policy or a cookie notice where required.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect some website functionality.

08

Sharing of Personal Data

We may share personal data with trusted service providers who help us operate the website and waitlist, such as:

  • website hosting providers;
  • form and database providers;
  • email delivery providers;
  • analytics providers;
  • customer communication tools;
  • security, anti-spam, or fraud prevention tools;
  • professional advisers such as lawyers, auditors, or accountants.

These service providers may process personal data only for the purposes we authorize and subject to appropriate contractual, security, and confidentiality obligations.

We may also disclose personal data if required by law, regulation, legal process, court order, government request, or to protect our rights, users, systems, or business.

We use Google Forms and related Google services to collect, store, and manage waitlist responses. Google may process personal data as our service provider or infrastructure provider for these purposes.

09

No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell your personal data.

We also do not share your waitlist personal data for cross-context behavioural advertising unless we specifically disclose this and provide any legally required opt-out rights.

10

International Transfers

We are based in India, but our service providers may process or store personal data in India, the United States, or other countries.

Where required, we will use appropriate safeguards for international transfers of personal data.

Because we use Google services for waitlist collection and management, your personal data may be processed or stored on infrastructure located outside your country, including outside India or the United States, depending on Google's systems, services, and account settings.

11

Data Retention

We retain waitlist personal data for as long as necessary to manage the waitlist, communicate with you, validate product demand, prepare for launch, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain business records.

Unless a longer period is required by law or legitimate business needs, we aim to delete or anonymize inactive waitlist personal data within 24 months from collection or within 12 months after product launch, whichever is earlier.

You may request deletion earlier by contacting us.

12

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

However, no website, network, or storage system is completely secure. You should avoid submitting sensitive or confidential information through the waitlist form.

13

Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to:

  • access your personal data;
  • correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data;
  • update your personal data;
  • request deletion of personal data;
  • withdraw consent;
  • object to or restrict certain processing;
  • request information about how your personal data is processed;
  • opt out of certain communications;
  • complain to a relevant authority where applicable.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@aquesa-solutions.com. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

14

India Privacy Rights

For users in India, we will provide a point of contact for questions and requests related to personal data.

You may contact us to request access, correction, updating, deletion, withdrawal of consent, or grievance redressal regarding your personal data.

15

United States Privacy Rights

For users in the United States, state privacy laws may provide additional rights depending on your state of residence and whether those laws apply to us.

These rights may include the right to know, access, correct, delete, obtain a copy of personal data, opt out of certain sharing or targeted advertising, and appeal certain decisions.

We do not currently sell waitlist personal data.

16

Children

Our website and waitlist are intended for users who are at least 18 years old.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

17

Data Breach Notification

If a personal data breach occurs and notification is required by applicable law, we will notify affected individuals and/or relevant authorities as required.

18

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

If we make material changes, we may provide additional notice, such as by email or website notice.
